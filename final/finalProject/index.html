How to Play

This game is a 2-player competetive game-whoever earns the most amount of points win the game. Note that stem color has no effect on the point value. There are 4 different types of points:

  • +1
  • -1

To earn points, the player must click on the images on the screen before your turn runs out.

  • +5
  • -5

For flower tops, they are randomly worth either +5 or -5. It is up to you whether or not you wish to take the chance.

There is a moment after the first player's turn to switch to the next player. And above all else, have fun!
