This game is a 2-player competetive game-whoever earns the most amount of points win the game. There are 4 different types of points:

  • +1
  • -1

To earn points, the player must click on the images on the screen before the time runs out.

  • +5
  • -5

For flower tops, they could be worth either +5 or -5. And above all else, have fun!
