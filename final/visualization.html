Usablity Test

The main thing I want to focus on is the UX/UI for my project. There will be two sets of people playing the game, a versus match between two casuals (games less than once per month) and a versus match between two competitives--a total of four testers. With these two groups, there will be two types of tests. The first test is a blind playthrough and just letting the players figure out the game and mechanics themselves. After this, I will explain anything they were confused about in the first playthrough and make them play again, observing any differences.

The interview questions are very open-ended:

  • Thoughts about the game experience
  • Things that could be better mechanically or in explanation
  • How are the visuals
  • Any other thoughts
